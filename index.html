THE CODE

❮ ❯

I took the scenic route towards data science.

I pivoted from a PhD in Neuromechanics. A large contributor to my cold feet: Getting my first taste of coding to analyze data for my MSc in Applied Health Sciences.

A cautious career pivot, I first deferred my PhD offer to work as a research assistant in a related field, healthcare. The experience of communicating research insights to patients and healthcare professionals was indispensable.

Still, something was missing for me...

What was missing in my roles was the opportunity to wrangle, analyze and visualize data. I wanted to learn to write code that would allow me to activate data.

Fast forward to me taking the PhD off the table and completing my Diploma in Data Science.

This career transformation hasn't been easy, but it has been worth it. I love coding so much that I completed a part-time web development course, while working as a full-time data science teaching assistant. I can't wait to use my skills to support a team in communicating data effectively and activating insights.
